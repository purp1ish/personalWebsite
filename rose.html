Rosé

This is my cat, Rosé. I started to take care of him when he was a kitten.

He is a very playful and affectionate cat.

Every morning, he wakes me up by jumping on my stomach.

Then I pet him and he purrs contentedly.

He is also very curious and loves to explore, he also likes strangers.

when strangers come to visit, he always chases after them. He is a very dog like cat.

I love him so much and I want to give the best to him.
